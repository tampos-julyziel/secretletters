How It Works

📝

1. Write from the Heart

Pour your feelings onto digital paper. Choose dynamic colors, beautiful fonts, and quirky stickers. Attach a photo or record a voice note to make it truly personal.

✈️

2. Fold & Send It Flying

Watch your letter transform into an origami plane and fly across the screen. Share the unique 5-character Flight Key with your favorite person.

💌

3. Catch & Unwrap

Your friend enters the Flight Key from any device, anywhere in the world. The plane lands, the letter unfolds — and your words come alive.
